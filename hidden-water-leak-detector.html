Find a Hidden Water Leak in Your Tyler Home (Water Meter Test)

Your water meter is the most reliable way to confirm a hidden leak. Tyler homes sit on slab foundations, so leaks often hide under the concrete with no visible sign until the bill spikes. Two meter readings and the form below tell you exactly how much water is escaping and what it costs at real Tyler Water Utilities rates.

How to run the meter test

  1. Turn off every water fixture and appliance in the home, including the irrigation system.
  2. Go to the meter box at the curb. Record the reading (gallons or cubic feet as shown on the face).
  3. Wait at least 2 hours without using any water.
  4. Return to the meter, record the second reading, and enter both numbers below.
