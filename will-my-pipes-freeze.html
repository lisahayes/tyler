Will My Pipes Freeze Tonight? Tyler TX Pipe Freeze Risk Tool

Tyler does not get Arctic winters, but when a hard freeze hits, the damage is worse than in colder climates because local pipes have little to no insulation and homes are not built to buffer sub-freezing air. Winter Storm Uri in February 2021 showed what happens when East Texas pipes are not protected. This tool tells you when to act and what to do.

If a pipe already froze or burst

  • Shut off the main water valve immediately.
  • Open a faucet to relieve pressure.
  • Do not use a torch or open flame to thaw.
  • Use a hair dryer or heating pad, starting near the faucet end.
  • Call a licensed Tyler plumber for the repair.
